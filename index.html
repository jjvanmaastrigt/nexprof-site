Our Approach

How We Work

Built on research.
Grounded in industry.

NexProf operates at the intersection of labour market research, vocational training, and employer demand. Our work is shaped by direct relationships with leading companies and academic institutions across the Netherlands and Europe.

Academic Research Partnerships

We collaborate with TU Delft, TU/e Eindhoven, and other leading research institutions to ground our career pathway methodology in evidence-based labour market analysis.

Employer Demand Intelligence

Ongoing dialogue with ASML, Philips, Royal HaskoningDHV, Heijmans, BAM, and others informs which technical roles face the greatest shortages and what hiring managers need.

Validated Training Pathways

Training recommendations are cross-referenced with accredited institutions, ROC/MBO programmes, and sector-specific certification bodies across the Netherlands and EU.
